Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using temporary phone numbers for SMS verification.

What is a temporary phone number?

A temporary phone number is a disposable virtual number that can receive SMS messages. It's perfect for signing up on websites that require phone verification without using your personal number.

Is this service free?

Yes! Our service is completely free to use. You can receive unlimited SMS messages on any of our available phone numbers without any registration or payment.

Are the messages private?

No, all messages received on our temporary numbers are public and can be seen by anyone. Do not use these numbers for sensitive information, banking, or personal accounts you want to keep secure.

How long do the numbers stay active?

Our phone numbers are rotated periodically to ensure availability. Numbers typically remain active for several weeks, but we recommend using them for one-time verifications rather than long-term accounts.

Which countries are supported?

We currently support phone numbers from 15 countries including the United States, United Kingdom, Germany, France, Japan, and more. Check our homepage for the full list of available countries.

Why didn't I receive my SMS?

Some services block virtual and VoIP numbers. If you don't receive your SMS within a few minutes, try using a number from a different country or a different number from the same country.

Can I send SMS from these numbers?

No, our temporary numbers can only receive SMS messages. They cannot be used to send outgoing messages or make/receive phone calls.

What should I use temporary numbers for?

Temporary numbers are ideal for: signing up on websites you don't fully trust, testing applications, avoiding spam, protecting your privacy online, and one-time verifications for promotions or trials.

What should I NOT use temporary numbers for?

Never use temporary numbers for: banking or financial services, important personal accounts (email, social media you want to keep), two-factor authentication on secure accounts, or any service where you need to recover your account later.

Do I need to register to use the service?

No registration required! Simply select a country, choose a phone number, and start receiving SMS messages immediately. It's that simple.
